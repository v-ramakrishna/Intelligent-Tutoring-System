Power Rule

Power Rule with any real number of n, multiply n with the exponent x and then subtract the exponent by one.

Power Rule:

(d/dx)xn = nxn-1


Power Rule Example(1):

Before Power Rule:

(d/dx)x2

After Power Rule:

2x1


Power Rule Example(2):

Before Power Rule:

(d/dx)5x4

After Power Rule:

20x3
